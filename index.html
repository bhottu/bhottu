Welcome to Mingle

Welcome to Mingle, a versatile classified web platform designed for all your buying and selling needs. Our application is built to handle a wide range of classified ads and integrates seamlessly with e-commerce functionalities to provide an all-in-one solution.

Features

  • Multipurpose Classified Ads: Post and browse ads across various categories including real estate, vehicles, electronics, and more.
  • Advanced Search & Filters: Find specific items or services quickly with our powerful search and filtering options.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Enjoy a seamless experience with our intuitive and easy-to-navigate design.
  • Discuss Feature: Engage in conversations with buyers and sellers directly within the app.
  • Maps and Location-Based Services: Discover local listings and connect with nearby sellers using integrated maps.
  • User Login and Registration: Securely create and manage user accounts with login and registration functionality.
  • Gmail Login: Simplify the login process with Gmail integration for easy access.
  • User Follow & Unfollow: Follow users to keep track of their listings and unfollow when needed.
  • Image and Video Uploads: Enhance your listings with multimedia by uploading images and videos.
  • Monetization with Google Adsense: Earn revenue by displaying Google Adsense ads on your website.
